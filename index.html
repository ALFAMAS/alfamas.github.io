// 04 :: About

I work in travel technology, building the systems that search, price, and book flights. That means integrating GDS platforms (Amadeus, Sabre, Travelport), implementing IATA NDC, connecting low-cost carriers, and wiring payment gateways such as CyberSource into booking flows.

Day to day that's API engineering: REST and SOAP integrations, authentication, idempotent order handling, and the operational concerns (rate limits, retries, observability) that keep bookings from failing quietly.

Outside the airline domain I build full-stack TypeScript systems: my zerotrust starter covers authentication (OAuth, passkeys, MFA), multi-tenant organizations, billing, and the security hardening that production SaaS needs.

  • Focus NDC/GDS integration, payments, booking systems
  • Stack TypeScript, Node.js, Hono, Next.js, PostgreSQL, Redis
  • Education Bachelor of Business (Information Systems / Hospitality Management), Canterbury Institute of Management, Sydney
  • Location Sydney, Australia
